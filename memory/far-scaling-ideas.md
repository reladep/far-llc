# 10 Ideas to Scale FAR to $1M ARR

## The Goal
5,000 users × $200/year = $1M ARR

---

## Ideas (Product Manager + CEO Perspective)

### 1. "Advisor Match" Quiz (Freemium Lead Magnet)
**What:** 30-second quiz: "Find your perfect advisor" — inputs: location, assets, goals. Outputs: top 3 matches.
**Why:** High-intent lead capture. Converts casual browsers to buyers.
**Data:** NerdWallet/Policygenius convert 3-5% of quiz users. 
**Build:** 5 question flow → algorithm → top 3 firm recommendations with FAR affiliate link.

---

### 2. "Fee Negotiator" Tool (Viral Feature)
**What:** User enters their current advisor's fee → FAR shows market rate + talking points for negotiation.
**Why:** High value, shareable. "My advisor is overcharging me!"
**Moat:** Your SEC data is the source of truth for "market rate."
**Build:** Simple form → compare to firm median → generated PDF with negotiation script.

---

### 3. "Wealth Transfer" Content Hub (SEO Engine)
**What:** $100T wealth transfer coming (Baby Boomers → Gen X/Millennials). Build content hub: "How to inherit wealth," "Questions to ask your inherited advisor."
**Why:** SEO domination. Long-tail keywords. Massive organic traffic.
**Data:** "wealth transfer" search volume growing 40% YoY.
**Build:** Blog + guides + "find advisor" CTAs. Target: "what to do with inherited money" keywords.

---

### 4. "Advisor Alerts" (Recurring Revenue)
**What:** Monitor your advisor — new SEC filings, AUM changes, fee changes, disclosure events.
**Why:** Sticky. Users come back weekly. $5-10/month.
**Build:** Email + push notifications. Automated from SEC data ingestion.

---

### 5. "Verified Reviews" (Trust/Network Effect)
**What:** User-submitted reviews of advisors (after engagement). Moderated, verified clients only.
**Why:** The missing piece — Glassdoor for wealth management. Trust + differentiation.
**Moat:** User content = network effects. Hard for competitors to replicate.
**Build:** Simple review form → moderation queue → public profiles.

---

### 6. "Flat-Fee Advisor Directory" (Differentiation)
**What:** Filter specifically for flat-fee, fee-only advisors (vs. AUM-based).
**Why:** Growing demand from fee-conscious consumers. Clear USP.
**Data:** Flag firms with flat-fee structures in your database. Make this a primary filter.

---

### 7. "Referral Engine" (Viral Loop)
**What:** Users refer friends → both get 1 month free. $20 value.
**Why:** Viral coefficient > 1. Low CAC.
**Build:** Simple referral program with unique links. Track in app.

---

### 8. "Advisor CRM Connect" (Enterprise Upsell)
**What:** Advisors pay to claim their profile, respond to leads, integrate with CRM.
**Why:** B2B revenue. Advisors pay $50-100/month for leads.
**Build:** "Claim your profile" flow → lead inbox → CRM integrations (HubSpot, Salesforce).

---

### 9. "State-of-Market Report" (Lead Magnet + Authority)
**What:** Quarterly report: "Average advisor fees by state," "Fastest growing advisory firms," "Fee trends."
**Why:** Media pickup. Backlinks. Authority builder. Lead magnet (email gate).
**Data:** You have the data — publish it.
**Build:** 10-page PDF, gated by email, promoted on social.

---

### 10. "Private Wealth Podcast Sponsorship" (Customer Acquisition)
**What:** Sponsor 3-5 podcasts in personal finance/wealth space ($5-15K/month).
**Why:** Targeted reach. High LTV customers listen to podcasts.
**Math:** 500 new users × $200 = $100K. If podcast costs $10K and converts 2% → 500 users → $100K revenue. 10x ROI.
**Build:** Identify top 10 podcasts (ChooseFI, Mad Fientist, Rational Reminder).

---

## Recommended Priority

**Month 1-3 (Get to $10K MRR):**
1. Advisor Alerts (quick build, recurring revenue)
2. Fee Negotiator Tool (viral, shareable)
3. Referral Engine (viral loop)

**Month 4-6 (Scale to $50K MRR):**
4. Advisor Match Quiz (lead capture)
5. Private Wealth Podcast Sponsorship (acquisition)
6. State-of-Market Report (authority + leads)

**Month 7-12 (Scale to $83K MRR = $1M ARR):**
7. Verified Reviews (moat)
8. Wealth Transfer Content Hub (SEO)
9. Flat-Fee Directory (differentiation)
10. Advisor CRM Connect (enterprise upsell)

---

## Key Metrics to Track

- **CAC:** Target < $30 (should be < 15% of LTV)
- **LTV:** $200 × 3 years = $600
- **Viral Coefficient:** Target > 1.1
- **Conversion:** Quiz → Paid: 3-5%
- **Churn:** Target < 5% monthly
