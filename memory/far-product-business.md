# FAR Product & Business Questions

## Product Strategy

### Two Views / Tiers

**Consumer View ($200/year)**
- Consumer-facing for finding a new wealth advisor
- Transparent fee structures
- SEC data (verified)
- Fee negotiation help

**Enterprise View ($1,000/year)**
- 1/10 cost of competitor platforms
- Includes all consumer info PLUS:
  - Valuation information
  - Custody details
  - CRM integrations (long-term)
  - More granular data

## Go-to-Market Pricing (Updated 2026-02-17)

### Beta Pricing — 3 Tiers

**Tier 1 — Data Access ($20/month, paid annually = $240/yr)**
- Full platform access: search, compare, alerts, fee data
- SEC-verified data, transparent fee structures

**Tier 2 — Advisory Intro ($50/month, paid annually = $600/yr)**
- Everything in Tier 1
- 1-on-1 intro call with Alex
- Curated list of 3 firm recommendations tailored to client
- Annual check-in call

**Tier 3 — Full Advisory (Custom pricing, scope-dependent)**
- Everything in Tier 2
- Portfolio reviews
- Asset allocation guidance
- Join client meetings with advisors
- Pricing based on scope/complexity
