# FAR — Feature List

## Core Features (MVP)

### 1. Search & Discovery
- **Search bar** — Keyword search for firm names, locations, specialties
- **Browse by category** — Popular searches, featured firms, "near you"
- **Filter system** —
  - Fee structure (flat fee, hourly, AUM %, commission)
  - AUM range
  - Minimum investment
  - Location / radius search
  - Specialties (estate planning, tax, FIRE, business owners, etc.)
  - Firm size (solo, small team, large)
  - Years in business
- **Sort options** — Alphabetical, AUM, growth rate, fees, rating

### 2. Firm Profiles
- Firm name, logo, location(s)
- Contact info (phone, email, website)
- Fee structure / pricing
- AUM (Assets Under Management)
- Year founded
- Team size / advisors
- Services offered
- Specialties
- Credentials / certifications (CFP, CPA, etc.)
- Custodian relationships
- Client types (individuals, families, businesses)
- "About" narrative
- Recent news / updates

### 3. Comparison Tool
- Side-by-side comparison (2-4 firms)
- Selectable fields to compare
- Highlight differences
- Save comparison (for logged-in users)

### 4. User Accounts
- **Guest** — Search, view, basic compare
- **Free account** — Save firms, save comparisons, email alerts
- **Saved list** — "My Firms" collection

### 5. Lead Capture / Connection
- "Request Info" form
- "Schedule Consultation" integration
- Share firm profile
- Email firm directly (anon until opted in)

### 6. Payments (for advisors / firms)
- **Firm subscription** — Monthly/annual fees for premium listings
- **Featured placement** — Pay to appear at top of search
- **Lead fees** — Pay per qualified lead
- **Featured profile badges** — "Promoted" or "Partner"
- Payment processing (Stripe, etc.)

### 7. Ratings & Reviews
- User ratings (1-5 stars)
- Written reviews
- Verification (only verified clients?)
- Firm response capability

### 8. Content / SEO / Blog
- **Blog articles** — Educational content (how to choose advisor, financial planning tips)
- **SEO pages** — Programmatic pages (e.g., "Best financial advisors in [City]")
- **Guides** — "How to choose an advisor", "Questions to ask"
- **FAQ** — Common questions answered
- **Glossary** — Financial terms defined
- **Newsletter signup,** — Email list weekly/monthly digest

### 9. Contact Page
- General inquiry form
- For users: Help / support
- For firms: Partnership / advertising inquiries
- Social media links
- Physical address (if applicable)

### 10. Legal / Disclosures
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service
- Disclaimer (not financial advice)
- Cookie Policy
- Compliance disclosures (SEC, FINRA references)
- "How we make money" disclosure (affiliate/sponsored listings)
