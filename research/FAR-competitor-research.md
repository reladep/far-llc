# FAR, LLC - Competitor / comparable landscape (living doc)

_Last updated: 2026-02-16_

## Key Pricing Insight
**B2B competitor pricing:** Most enterprise platforms (AdvizorPro, FINTRX, Discovery Data) cost **$5,000–$20,000/year** — opaque pricing that puts small/mid-sized teams at a disadvantage.

**Dakota FA** disrupts this at **$995/year** for a single license — significantly more affordable.

**FAR opportunity:** Consumer-facing free tier with paid upgrades could capture price-sensitive users that enterprise tools ignore.

## FAR positioning (working)
A **consumer-facing comparison platform** for wealth management firms - like Glassdoor meets NerdWallet. Helps people **understand, vet, and compare** firms side-by-side on fees, performance, specialties, growth signals, and client fit.

**Future B2B optionality:** CRM integrations, advisor lookup APIs, data licensing (keep door open but don't build yet).

## What counts as a "competitor" (buckets)
1) **Advisor/firm discovery & matching (consumer-facing)** - helps users find an advisor/firm.
2) **RIA/firm data platforms (B2B / industry)** - Form ADV-derived databases, firmographics, contact data, growth signals.
3) **Rankings & editorial lists** - useful for traffic + credibility; sometimes interactive.
4) **Regulatory + public data sources** - baseline truth sources (not competitors but inputs).
5) **Review / lead-gen marketplaces** - may monetize via lead sales.

## Initial short list (seed)

### 1) Advisor/firm discovery & matching (consumer)

- **AdvisorFinder** - assessment-based advisor discovery; filters by location, specialties, fee structures. User controls who receives their data (vs lead-gen selling).
  - Source: https://advisorfinder.com/
  - Model: Free for consumers, lead-gen or subscription for advisors

- **SmartAsset - SmartAdvisor Match** - questionnaire → matched with up to 3 pre-screened advisors. Strong SEO + content play.
  - Source: https://smartadvisormatch.com/
  - Model: Lead-gen (advisors pay for matched leads)

- **Paladin Registry** - Consumer matching since 2004. All advisors are fiduciaries. Validates/credentials + documents business practices for users.
  - Source: https://www.paladinregistry.com/
  - Model: Lead-gen + subscription

- **Harness Wealth (now Harness.co)** - SEC-registered adviser that refers clients to third-party investment managers. Acts as matching layer.
  - Source: https://www.harness.co/
  - Model: Advisory/referral fees

- **Zoe Financial** - B2B platform play (not pure consumer matching). Modern unified platform for advisors + clients. Partners with independent fiduciary advisors.
  - Source: https://zoefinancial.com/
  - Model: B2B SaaS/platform

### 2) RIA / firm data platforms (industry)
- **ISS Market Intelligence — MarketPro (powered by Discovery Data)** — distribution/recruiting intelligence + advisor/firm data; segmentation/filters; mentions tracking advisor movement weekly; enrichment + integrations.
  - Source: https://www.issmarketintelligence.com/solutions/marketpro/
  - Source: https://www.issmarketintelligence.com/solutions/marketpro/marketpro-powered-by-discovery-data/
  - **Pricing:** $5,000–$20,000/year (enterprise)

- **AdvizorPro** — RIA database built from Form ADV + firm intelligence; publishes "Top 100 Independent RIAs 2026" interactive ranking.
  - Source: https://advizorpro.com/post/top-independent-rias
  - **Pricing:** $5,000–$20,000/year

- **FINTRX** — private wealth intelligence platform: RIA/Broker-Dealer/Family Office/Endowment/Foundation datasets; emphasizes "AI-powered intelligence" and large coverage + monthly updates; free trial.
  - Source: https://www.fintrx.com/
  - **Pricing:** $5,000–$20,000/year (enterprise)

- **Discovery Data** — extensive institutional coverage (advisors, broker-dealers, investment firms); part of ISS Market Intelligence.
  - Source: https://www.issmarketintelligence.com/solutions/marketpro/
  - **Pricing:** $5,000–$20,000/year

- **Dakota** — RIA database + marketplace. "All-in-one platform for advisor relationships." Combines firm data with recruiting/marketplace features.
  - Source: https://www.dakota.com/
  - **Pricing:** $995/year (single license) for FA database; enterprise pricing higher

- **Dakota FA Database** — 200K+ financial advisors at 75+ broker-dealers and banks. Detailed contact info, bio, team structure, daily enrichment by 60+ person data team.
  - Source: https://www.dakota.com/resources/blog/the-top-financial-advisor-databases-for-2025
  - **Pricing:** $995/year (single license) — notably more affordable than enterprise players

- **RIA Database** — Detailed data on every financial advisor nationwide. Quarterly security holdings data, mapped to RIA firms. Weekly updates.
  - Source: https://www.riadatabase.com/
  - Model: Subscription (database subscriptions)

### 2) RIA / firm data platforms (B2B) - expanded

  - Source: https://www.dakota.com/

### 3) Rankings & editorial (consumer)
- **NerdWallet - Best Financial Advisors** - Editorial "best of" lists with fee/mimum comparisons. Strong SEO.
  - Source: https://www.nerdwallet.com/financial-advisors/learn/best-financial-advisors
  - Model: Affiliate + content

- **Bankrate - Best Financial Advisors** - Editorial rankings, fee info.
  - Source: https://www.bankrate.com/investing/financial-advisors/best-financial-advisors/
  - Model: Affiliate + content

- **Unbiased** - Advisor discovery + editorial. "Compare features, pricing, pros/cons."
  - Source: https://www.unbiased.com/
  - Model: Lead-gen + content

- **Wealthtender** - Advisor reviews with verified client reviews. "Compare 750+ fee-only fiduciary advisors."
  - Source: https://wealthtender.com/
  - Model: Lead-gen + reviews

- **Financial Planning - RIA Leaders** (rankings by AUM) - editorial list.
  - Source: https://www.financial-planning.com/list/ria-leaders-2025-top-20-firms-by-aum

### 4) Regulatory / public data sources (inputs)
- **SEC Investment Adviser Statistics** (market sizing + counts; potential baseline reference).
  - Mentioned via AdvizorPro article linking to SEC: https://www.sec.gov/data-research/statistics-data-visualizations/investment-adviser-statistics

## FAR research worksheet (recommended columns)
For each comparable platform, capture:
- Name + URL
- Primary user (consumer / advisor / asset manager / recruiter / vendor)
- Core value prop
- Data sources (Form ADV, surveys, scraping, partnerships)
- Pricing model (subscription / lead fee / listing fee / freemium / ads)
- Coverage depth (firm-level, advisor-level, both)
- Comparison features (fees, AUM, growth, specialties, custody, etc.)
- Freshness / update cadence
- SEO strategy (programmatic pages? editorial?)
- Notes (design patterns, onboarding, trust signals)
