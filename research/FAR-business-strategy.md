## Proprietary Data & Moat

**Goal:** Make the platform harder to replicate

### Sources of Proprietary Value:
- **Synthesized scores** — Aggregate metrics that combine multiple data points
- **Data abstraction** — Transform raw SEC filings into digestible insights
- **Unique UX** — How we present and compare data

### Types of Data to Add (Day 1 Priority):

| Category | Data Points |
|----------|-------------|
| **Litigation** | SEC/CFTC violations, court rulings, regulatory actions |
| **Alerts** | Recent regulatory updates, form amendments |
| **Disclosure Scores** | Clean vs. flagged disclosure history |
| **Growth Tags** | High growth, slow growth, negative client growth, employee growth trends |
| **Firm Health Indicators** | Revenue per employee, client retention signals |

## Incremental Workflows

**Question:** How to structure progressive momentum?

### Approaches:
1. **Weekly data drops** — New data categories release on a cadence
2. **Beta community** — Early access users who provide feedback
3. **Feature flags** — Roll out incrementally, measure engagement
4. **A/B testing** — Test UX approaches with real users
5. **Content flywheel** — Blog posts / guides that drive SEO while product builds
